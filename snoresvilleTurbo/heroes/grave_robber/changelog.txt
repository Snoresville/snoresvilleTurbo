----------< Grave Robber >----------
Crit Effect:
Crits now also give bonus crit chance
In addition, the Grave Robber heals 10 HP and 7 Stress.

Pick to the Face:
Can now cause Prot debuff
Debuff chance | 100/110/120/130/140%

Damage | -15/-15/-15/-15/-15% -> 0/20/40/60/80

Lunge:
Now can target the 4th enemy rank.
Adds the Riposte effect on the Grave Robber that lasts for the duration of the battle.

Flashing Daggers:
Can now be used from any rank and can affect all enemies in every rank.
Damage | -33/-33/-33/-33/-33% -> 0/0/0/0/0%

Shadow Fade:
With the power of friendship, Grave Robber's crit, damage buff and speed gain increases dependent on party size.
Now stealths the entire party for two rounds.

Thrown Dagger:
Now can target the 1st enemy rank.

Accuracy | 90/95/100/105/110 -> 100/110/120/130/140

Poison Darts:
Now targets enemies in all ranks.

Toxin Trickery:
With the power of friendship, Graverobber's dodge and speed gain increases dependent on party size.
No battle limit.

Riposte Effect:
Graverobber will attack any attacking enemies while under the Riposte effect with Level 5 Thrown dagger.
